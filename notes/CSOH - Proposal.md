# Citizen Steve’s Oval Hours

A weekly public dialogue series held Wednesdays from 1pm to 2pm on the OSU Oval. Each session features a fifteen to twenty minute opening address on a provocative civic question, followed by open discussion and Q and A with attendees. The series draws on evidence from across the political spectrum to examine five urgent questions facing American democracy: 

1. Has Donald Trump started an illegal and immoral war in Iran?  
2. Is Donald Trump corrupting the presidency to enrich himself?  
3. Is Donald Trump covering up for the criminal Epstein network?  
4. Is Donald Trump creating a secret racist police force?  
5. Is Donald Trump trying to become a king?

Sessions will be live-streamed to YouTube and accompanied by a dedicated website featuring supplementary materials and resources.

Note Citizen Steve, PhD is speaking only as a private citizen, not as faculty of THE Ohio State University. All supplies, including the printing and copying of flyers, are being purchased with personal funds.
