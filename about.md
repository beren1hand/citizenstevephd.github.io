# 📜 About Citizen Steve PhD

## The Mission

**Citizen Steve PhD** provides evidence-based education examining critical questions about American democracy and constitutional governance.

## The Format

Five 90-minute educational sessions, designed with:
- **PhD-level rigor** in accessible presentation
- **Question-driven titles** that invite exploration
- **Conservative sourcing strategy** (Fox News, court records, Trump's own appointees)
- **Constitutional grounding** using founding principles

## The Approach

**Not:** Telling you what to think  
**But:** Providing evidence so you can decide for yourself

**Not:** Partisan attacks  
**But:** Pattern recognition using founders' own standards

**Not:** Abstract theory  
**But:** Documented evidence with sources

## The Sessions

Each session examines one domain where current governance patterns may match the monarchical behaviors America's founders rejected:

1. **War Powers** - Constitutional authority, Just War Theory
2. **Corruption** - Emoluments, family enrichment
3. **Justice** - Equal application of law, accountability
4. **Enforcement** - Due process, judicial oversight
5. **Elections** - Democratic transfer of power, constitutional remedies

## The Goal

To answer one question: **Is Donald Trump trying to become king?**

Using the founders' own test (Declaration of Independence grievances against King George III), we examine whether current patterns match historical tyranny.

Then we discuss what citizens can demand: **Constitutional processes that would reveal the truth.**
